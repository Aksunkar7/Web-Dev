Buckwheat with meat

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Meat 400 g
Seasoning A half of pack
Buckwheat 2 mugs

Step 1: Fry the meat a quarter of an hour.
Step 2: Pour the some water and close the pan for 5 minutes.
Step 3: Put the buckwheat and pour all of the heated water, then close the pan for one third hour.
